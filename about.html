Our Story

COMP3612 Assign 1 began with a simple belief: that every programmer deserves clothes that not only look exceptional but feel extraordinary. Founded in 2025 by Jack Le, our company emerged from a shared frustration with the compromise between quality and affordability in the coding attire market, as well as a strong desire to get an A grade in COMP3612.

Our Values

Quality First

We source only the finest materials. Every stitch, every button, every detail is carefully considered in a robust Linux shell

Sustainability

We're committed to responsible manufacturing practices, using eco-friendly materials, and ensuring our production processes minimize environmental impact

Innovation

While we respect traditional tailoring methods, we continuously explore new assembly language techniques to improve comfort, durability, and style.
